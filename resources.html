Microgrants

TechShift offers microgrants to member organizations to support campus tech for social good initiatives. These events can range from small get togethers, to semester or year long programs.

Any organization which has been accepted as a member of the TechShift Alliance is eligible to apply!

Microgrants are generally limited to $150, but depending on the request, amount of detail provided, and budget, we are happy to offer larger sums.

You can learn more about this program on our Microgrants FAQ page.

Organizational Mentorship

For all new member organizations, TechShift will assign an alumni mentor who will be able to provide key insight on starting and growing your new organization.

To New Member Orgs:

  • Once you are assigned to your mentor, you can decide when to meet, how often to meet, and what you talk about!
  • Topics for meetings can range from defining your organization’s mission statement to sourcing good web development tutorials.
  • In addition to your mentor, you will receive an extensive New Org Handbook with tons of details on organizational leadership, how to develop projects, branding and marketing, and much more!

To Mentors

  • Are you a former TechShift member interested in volunteering as an alumni mentor? Sign up here.
  • Topics for meetings can range from defining your organization’s mission statement to sourcing good web development tutorials.
  • New to mentoring? Not to fret! We will provide you with a mentor handbook that will familiarize you with all the resources that we offer, and some of the most common struggles of starting organizations.

Technical Mentorship

Several of our organizations develop projects for local nonprofits who lack the technological expertise and/or budget to develop them on their own.

Although many of the students working on these projects are currently studying computer science, we are always in need of technical mentors who can share their knowledge and experience with our students.

If you are an experienced programmer or have industry experience that you'd be willing to share, please fill out this form.
